## 📖 About The Project

The **Land of Love Foundation** website was developed as a **freelance project** to establish a professional digital presence for the NGO. The website effectively showcases the foundation's mission, initiatives, volunteer opportunities, donation campaigns, and community activities through a modern, responsive, and user-friendly interface.

About Us

Land of Love Foundation

Land of Love Foundation is a registered non-profit organisation founded in 2021 by J.M. Balaji. We support education, skill development, employment, health, blood network assistance and food distribution in Tamil Nadu and Kerala.

We are committed to serving people with dignity, compassion and without discrimination.
